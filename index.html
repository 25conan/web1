Beethoven's Violin Sonata 는 어떤 음악인가?

Ludwig van Beethoven composed the following violin sonatas between 1798 and 1812.

  • Violin Sonata in A major (Beethoven), Hess 46 (fragmentary)
  • Violin Sonata No. 1 in D, Op. 12
  • Violin Sonata No. 2 in A, Op. 12
  • Violin Sonata No. 3 in E-flat, Op. 12
  • Violin Sonata No. 4 in A minor, Op. 23
  • Violin Sonata No. 5 in F, Op. 24 ("Spring")
  • Violin Sonata No. 6 in A, Op. 30
  • Violin Sonata No. 7 in C minor, Op. 30
  • Violin Sonata No. 8 in G, Op. 30
  • Violin Sonata No. 9 in A, Op. 47 ("Kreutzer")
  • Violin Sonata No. 10 in G, Op. 96
